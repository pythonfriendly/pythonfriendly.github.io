About SAATHI

SAATHI is an AI-powered mental wellness companion designed specifically for Indian youth.

  • 24x7 confidential and stigma-free support
  • Emotion-aware chatbot with personalized coping tips
  • Multilingual support across Indian languages
  • Optional escalation to verified counselors
  • Gamified wellness journey with mood tracking

Our goal is to make mental wellness accessible, friendly, and culturally relevant.
